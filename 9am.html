The 9am Project

9am Origins

The 9am Project began as a simple idea: capture the world at the same moment every day. Originally, it focused on photographing silos — tall, quiet structures that stand against the sky like markers of time. What started as a creative experiment slowly became a ritual, a way to pause, breathe, and notice the world.
